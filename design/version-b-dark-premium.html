Process

Three things no other
travel app does together.

01
Social trend signals
X/Twitter API scans current chatter about your destination — scored by volume, recency, and engagement. Sponsored content is filtered out automatically.
X API · scored · filtered
02
Live stop verification
Foursquare checks opening hours. Open-Meteo checks weather. All during generation — not after. Stops that don't pass are replaced before you see them.
Foursquare · Open-Meteo
03
Per-stop reason codes
Every stop carries typed chips explaining the selection logic. Not a paragraph — structured signals you can scan in two seconds.
social · transport · budget
